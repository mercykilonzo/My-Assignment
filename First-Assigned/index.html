My First day at AkiraChix

I reported at AkiraChix on 1st of Ferbruary, which was on a staruday. The first week was between 3rd to 7th of February.

Activities in week one

The week began with a meeting with the administration. This meeting was to help us familiarize with the environment. After which we went to our respective classes were everyone had to find their sitting places and laptop. This was easy since each chair and laptop had the students code on it. Then we had to confirm if our laptops were okay and operating as required.

During the same week, we had afew classes which were an introduction to some of the items we will use during the program like; google docs, spreadsheets, git, slides and email. This gave us a chance to meet some of the trainers who will be taking us through the different units.

Towards the end of the week, we had another meeting with Nancy and Wilter. They shared with us the timetable and routine for both the weekdays and weekend.

List of the good things experienced

Below is a list things i have experienced so far:

  1. AkiraChix has a warm and welcoming environment.
  2. They provide all essential items.
  3. The food here is very good.
  4. The trainers ensure the class is interactive and all everyone is on the same page.

List of the not good things

Below is a list of the not good things i have experienced;

  • The weather here is very cold
  • Waking up early
